Items for which Central assistance under the Centrally Sponsored Scheme for implementation of the Protection of Civil Rights Act, 1955 and the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of Atrocities) Act, 1989 is proposed for the year 2023-24.


(i) Awareness generation and setting up of awareness Centres, organizing workshops (Rule 3(viii) of PoA Rules), Training Camps, Sadbhavana shivirs, essay and other competitions,any other alike item.





(ii) Publicity
(iii) Legal aid to persons subjected to any disability arising out of “untouchability”/subjected to atrocities. (Section 15A 2(i) of the PCR Act and Section 21 2(i) of the PoA Act).




(iv) Traveling and maintenance expenses to witnesses and victims of atrocities, during investigation and trial of offences(Section 21 2(i) of the PoA Act and Rule 11 of the PoA Rules).




(v) Economic and social rehabilitation of victims of atrocities (Section 21 2(iii) of the PoA Act and Rule 12(4) of the PoA Rules).




(vi) Periodic survey of the working of the provisions of the PCR & PoA Act (Section 15(2) (v) of the PCR Act, 21(2) (vi) of the PoA Act & Rule 8 of the PoA Rules).
(vii) Identification of the areas where persons are under any disability arising out of “untouchability”/ members of SC/ST are subjected to any atrocities (Section 15(2) (vi) of the PCR Act, 21(2) (vii) of the PoA Act & Rule 3(i) of the PoA Rules.
(viii) Awards for best performing gram-panchayats/ Panchayat Raj Institutions etc to eradicate untouchability and combat offences of atrocities.
(ix) Incentive for inter-caste marriages, where one of the spouses is a member of a Scheduled Caste.
